Founder Story

I'm Sahil Singh, Founder of MammthAI Investing Inc. For 10+years, I worked in Data & Analytics whilst learning how investing really works! In the beginning of my investing journey like many new investors, I poured my hard-earned savings into the market, only to face a steep learning curve filled with confusion, overwhelming information, and a lack of clear, actionable guidance. Through trial and error, I developed a framework that provided sustained returns, blending data-driven insights with practical investing strategies. But one question kept nagging at me: Why isn't there an app that simplifies this process for everyone? That question sparked a mission to make stock market investing accessible and empowering for all.

Determined to turn this vision into reality, I teamed up with my cousin, who joined as co-founder, and together we embarked on the rollercoaster of building a fintech startup. Through every hurdle, our shared passion for democratizing investing kept us going. We wanted to build more than just an app, rather create a tool that would give investors, novice and experienced alike, the clarity and confidence to make smarter financial decisions.

A pivotal moment in our journey came with the guidance of Mr. Michael Derchin, whose investing wisdom profoundly shaped our app's approach. His insights helped us refine our AI-driven tools, ensuring that MammthAI Investing delivers not just data, but actionable, user-friendly insights: what to buy & when to buy. Today, MammthAI Investing stands as a testament to our mission: to improve financial literacy & build generational wealth.
